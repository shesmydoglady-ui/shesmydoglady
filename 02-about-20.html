What Makes Us Different

We specialize in the dogs others find difficult

Reactive, fearful, high-energy these dogs don't need less care. They need the right care.

01

Reactive Dogs

We help you feel confident and in control walking your reactive or barking dog, at a pace that works for both of you.

02

Working Breeds

High-drive dogs need structure and purpose. We provide the outlets and training they thrive on.

03

Rescue Support

Rescues often carry history. We meet them with patience and help them settle into their new life.

04

Fearful Pups

Fear-based behavior requires a soft touch and consistent approach. We go at the dog's pace, always.

05

Puppy Foundation

Early socialization and routine-building that sets your puppy up for a confident, happy life.

06

Dogs in Training

We reinforce the work you're already doing consistent handling across every session and stay.
